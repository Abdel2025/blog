WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2021, Morocco USA Education is an educational Consulting Centre that assists
Moroccan students who are seeking to continue their higher education in the United States of America.
Our job is to assist them in the process starting from the very first step all the way to the final stage.

What is Special About MUE

School Preference

We will assist you in applying to the American University of your Choice. MUE agents will find you the school that meets your needs.

Visa Preparation

We work with students to prepare and get their student visas approved. MUE has enough expertise to help you with your visa.

Documents & Process

We assist you in preparing and submitting every document needed in the process. MUE agents will help you throughout the whole process.
