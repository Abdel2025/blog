WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2021, Morocco USA Education is an educational Consulting Centre that assists
Moroccan students who are seeking to continue their higher education in the United States of America.
We believe that students in Morocco are very talented and skilled, and they deserve to have
the opportunity to study in an American university of their choice. Our job is to assist them
in the process starting from the very first step all the way to the final stage.

What is Special About MUE

We will assist you apply to the American University of your Choice. MUE agents will look up the best school that meets your requirements.

We work with students to prepare and get their student visas approved. MUE has enough expertise to prepare you for the visa interview.

We assist you in preparing and submitting every document needed in the process. MUE agents will help you define the documents you need.

With over 13 years of experience in the American Higher Educational System, I built the skills and tools to help you complete and submit an attractive application to the University/College of your choice.
