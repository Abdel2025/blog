How to write an essay for college admission?

It’s not an easy task to write a good essay for your school admission as an international student, it requires time and writing skills, especially that English is not your native language, and you need to write an academic essay, not just a normal essay.

But it is also a chance where you can test your skills and practice writing in an academic way, here are a few points to keep in mind when writing an essay for college admission:

  • 1) Start by brainstorming ideas: start by asking yourself a few questions such as, what makes you a unique international student? What are some of the special experiences you had in the past that shaped your personality?

  • 2) Select a topic: think of a subject that will help you highlight your personality, thoughts, values, and goals in an animated way.

  • 3) Build a solid thesis statement: you should write a clear thesis statement that expresses the main idea of your essay.

  • 4) Have an outline for your essay: by having an outline, you are making it easy for yourself to organize the structure for your essay, which should have three parts: an introduction, body paragraphs, and a conclusion.

  • 5) Write an attractive introduction: you should write an introduction that’s attractive, which will grab the reader's attention, and allow them to guess what the essay is about.

  • 6) Mention realistic examples: using certain examples from your life to explain your points is going to make your essay more credible.

  • 7) Proofread: it’s always a good idea to proofread your essay after you finished writing it, to make sure it’s free from spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors. And it’s also good to see how another person is going to perceive it.
