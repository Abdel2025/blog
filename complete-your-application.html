Step 3: Complete Your Application

After choosing the universities you are interested in; the next step is to contact the Office of Admissions at each school and request an admissions application for graduate study. Each university and college in the United States establishes its own admissions requirements and selects applicants who meet them. As a result, each university or college will require a separate application.

After getting the admission application, and knowing what the required documents of each school are, make arrangements to have all of your secondary and post-secondary records sent straight to the schools you've chosen. Then, following the directions in each application packet, send in the applications and application fees.
